FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make a booking?

If you would like to make a booking, please send all of your details, including the date, venue, times etc. of the wedding or function, to info@goodmood.cat or call on +34 625 171 701.

Are there any other charges involved?

The price is fixed unless you change your arrangements, so the answer is NO. When making your booking we will take the start and finish time and the location into account and charge accordingly.

What events do you have experience in?

All of us are talented musicians, we've worked in many differetn bands, music styles and hundreds of different venues and settings. We have played in so many weddings, and we also have experience playing at corporate events, parties, product launches, award ceremonies, conferences, formal dinners, engagements, and more.

Do you offer Ceremony, Cocktails and Reception Music?

Of course! With the Good Mood Band you aare covered from the pre-ceremony, welcoming your guests to the last song at night with a saxophonist making you all dance along the DJ, we are extremely versatile and have you covered.

What do the musicians wear?

We choose what to wear depending on the event and what the client requires, but we love wearing formal, we believe that it is better to be over dressed than under-dressed

Can you provide the sound equipment

Unless it's strictly acoustic, our performances require amplification, the Good Mood Band can provide it but we are also open to plug into the speakers / sound equipment already in place, we'll communicate with the sound engineer before the daya of the event to make sure everything is good.

How long do you require to set up?

We usually arrive in place about 45 minutes before the show, in order to have enough time to set up, you don't pay for this time.
