Services

Events

Performing at events is our perfect match, we play the songs and covers you love, international music and spanish music, classsics and modern ones, we adapt the music depending on the taste of the client and the momentum. Our show at an event can be as Solosit, Duo or Full Band.

  • Corporate Events

    Businesses love to have live music at their events, tell us what's you idea and we'll make it happen, READ MORE...

    A professional band at a corporaate event, that's what we are talking about when it comes to hiring the Good Mood Covers Band for an event. We are a versatile team with focus on the client and their requirements, adapting our performance and our show to the event.

    Our music is perfect for a local company or an international one, we play mostly international english songs, but also spanish music, catalan and french, we love creating a cool atmosphere that engages with everyone.

    When booking the Good Mood Band you're also hiring professionality and quality, we provide our own sound system for the best performance and audio quality.

  • Private Events

    We adapt out show to the event, playing live music also at Private parties, READ MORE...

    We have played at Private Parties all over Barcelona and also in Girona, Tarragona and Lleida, all over the major cities in Catalonia and Spain.

    Our international music will adapt to the event, allowing us to play shows internationally, we've been to Andorra, Toulouse, Perpignan, Milan and even Paris! Anywhere music take us, we go.

    Get in touch with us if you want to book our music services and live band for your private party, we'll provide a quotation and all the info you need, also any questions are welcome, please ask us!

  • Hotels & Restaurants

    We haven't played at every restaurant, but we've been at the best ones in Barcelona, READ MORE...

    Our band is versatile, going from a Solo artist to a Full Band, and restaurants love that, we've added live music at the best restaurants in Barcelona.

    We play our solo show on gutiar, saxophone anc vocals, also as a Duo or even Full Band, mostly when it comes to events, that's our secret, we can offer what the client requires.

    At the booking please let us know what's you interest, and we'll provice the exact prices all included.
